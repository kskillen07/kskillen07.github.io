The Northern Ireland Housing Executive’s Children and Young People Programme aims to develop six networks across the greater Belfast area for meaningful, purposeful and sustainable cross community engagement aimed at children and young people in Belfast.
Find out more today by contacting the Peace IV Programme Good Relations Officer, Rachel Clarke rachelclarke2@nihe.gov.uk
This project is part of Belfast’s PEACE IV Action Plan which is financed through the European Union and managed by the Special EU Programmes Body.
