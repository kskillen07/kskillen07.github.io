Background to enirgy.info’s website

This website, which is independently-owned and operated, is aimed by enirgy.info at providing information about the Northern Ireland energy market. It will carry news, identify trends and and present analysis about issues which affect the supply and price of fuel. The enirgy.info website offers households in Northern Ireland the opportunity to compare prices charged by domestic electricity suppliers.

It is not designed nor intended to compare prices of all the domestic electricity products offered by all the electricity supply companies in Northern Ireland. In particular, some products and some suppliers are excluded from the price comparison. The price comparison information is not tailored to your individual needs.
