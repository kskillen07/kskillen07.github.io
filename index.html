About ENIRGY

Welcome to enirgy.info, the site that aims to inform you about the electricity market and more importantly how to cut your power bills.

enirgy.info is an initiative by local business journalist Jamie Delargy who, over the years, has made the subject of electricity and gas an area of special interest.

enirgy.info is independently owned, managed and funded by local company Delargy Productions. It receives no ‘click through” fees, advertising revenue or other payments from the electricity companies whose prices it compares.
