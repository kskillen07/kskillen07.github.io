What does this mean?

Many homes will use around 3200 units of electricity a year (average). But single households or couples in a flat could consume as few as 2100 units (low) while big families or those who live in large houses might use up to 5100 units annually (high). Choose whichever is closest to your situation. It will affect the calculation of your annual bill.
